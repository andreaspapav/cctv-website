CCTV Analysis for Camden Concil

Abstract

The area of Camden its well known for its high levels of criminal activity. The project assigned to our team is related with the CCTV monitoring of the whole Camden Borough. Our client is the Camden Council, they asked us to develop a software that will be used to monitor all the CCTVs in Camden area. The software should integrate AI (Artificial Intelligence) and AR(Agmented Reality) in order to notice any anti-social behaviour captured by the cameras.

What is our solution?

We will build a web application which can automatically detect the situations which require CCTV operators' attention. These situations include crime, street sleeping and accident etc. The CCTV operators will then deploy a specific team to solve the situation displayed on the screen. The web application contains the AI and AR to allow the auto-detection on any anti-social behaviour, and then categorize them into different emergency levels.
